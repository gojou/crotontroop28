First Annual Troop 28 Chili Cook-off Rules

What's a chili?

For this competition it's basically anything that includes chili peppers and looks like chili. It can be veggie/vegan. It can be white. It can be paleo. If you can find a way to work nuts into it knock yourself out. Because we are being so loose we require that you prominently display a list of your ingredients; we don't want anyone with medical, ethical, or aesthetic priorities eating something that might give them issues.

How the Competition will be Scored

Everyone who attends will get a ballot at the door. Each person will be allowed to vote on their three favorite chilis in rank order. Each vote for 1st will count as three checks for that chili, each for 2nd as two, each for 3rd as one. Most checks at the end of the competition wins the trophy. In the end there can be only one.

  1. Bring one gallon of chili. (What's left over you can keep, or you can let people take home in take-out containers, or you can donate it to feed the less fortunate.)
  2. BYO crock pot / instant pot / whatever. Also, bring your own serving utensils. We will provide bowls and cuttlery.
  3. Print out a list of ingredients so people know what they're getting.
  4. BYO Condiments. (Sharing is nice, though.)
  5. Make a sign for your entry. Have fun, be creative, make sure if you win people know you won.
